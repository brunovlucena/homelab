## Market Overview

### Recife, Brazil
- **Population**: ~1.6 million (metro: ~4 million)
- **GDP**: $25 billion
- **Healthcare Market**: Large public and private hospital network
- **Retail Market**: Growing fast-food and gas station chains
- **Language**: Portuguese
- **Currency**: Brazilian Real (BRL)
- **Advantages**: Lower competition, growing market, local connections

### Boston, Massachusetts
- **Population**: ~700,000 (metro: ~4.9 million)
- **GDP**: $500+ billion
- **Healthcare Market**: World-class hospitals, medical innovation hub
- **Retail Market**: Dense urban market, high foot traffic
- **Language**: English
- **Currency**: US Dollar (USD)
- **Advantages**: Higher budgets, tech-savvy, established market
