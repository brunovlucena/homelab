## Executive Summary

YouTube is the second-largest search engine and the primary platform for educational video content. This strategy focuses on **educational content**, **technical tutorials**, and **thought leadership** to establish authority in the AI automation space and drive qualified traffic to lucena.cloud.

**Key Objectives**:
1. **Authority Building**: Establish Bruno Lucena as the go-to expert for AI agent automation
2. **Traffic Generation**: Drive 5,000+ monthly website visits from YouTube
3. **Lead Generation**: 100+ qualified leads per quarter
4. **Subscriber Growth**: 1,000+ subscribers in first 6 months, 5,000+ in first year
5. **Business Development**: 5-10 closed deals per quarter from YouTube

## Target Audience

#### 2. Restaurant Operations Managers
- **Demographics**: 30-50 years old, Hospitality/Operations backgrounds
- **Pain Points**: Customer service automation, operations optimization, staff efficiency
- **Search Behavior**: "restaurant AI", "dining automation", "customer service AI"
- **Content Preference**: ROI-focused, demo videos, implementation guides

#### 3. DevOps/SRE Engineers
- **Demographics**: 28-45 years old, Engineering backgrounds
- **Pain Points**: Incident response, infrastructure automation, observability
- **Search Behavior**: "DevOps automation", "AI for SRE", "Kubernetes automation"
- **Content Preference**: Technical tutorials, architecture deep-dives, code walkthroughs

#### 4. E-Commerce Directors
- **Demographics**: 30-50 years old, E-commerce/Retail backgrounds
- **Pain Points**: Customer service, personalization, multi-channel sales
- **Search Behavior**: "e-commerce AI", "WhatsApp automation", "customer service bots"
- **Content Preference**: Implementation guides, ROI calculators, demo videos

- **Demographics**: 32-55 years old, Cybersecurity backgrounds

## Production Workflow

### Pre-Production (2-3 hours/video)

1. **Topic Selection**: Based on keyword research, audience requests
2. **Script/Outline**: Write detailed script or outline
3. **Visuals Preparation**: Prepare slides, diagrams, code examples
4. **Equipment Check**: Camera, microphone, lighting, screen recording setup

### Production (1-2 hours/video)

1. **Recording**: Screen recording or talking head
2. **Audio**: Record clear voice-over
3. **B-Roll**: Additional footage if needed

### Post-Production (2-4 hours/video)

1. **Editing**: Cut, trim, add transitions
2. **Graphics**: Add text overlays, graphics, branding
3. **Color Grading**: Enhance video quality
4. **Audio Mixing**: Balance audio levels
5. **Subtitles**: Add closed captions (crucial for accessibility and SEO)

### Publishing (30 minutes/video)

1. **Upload**: Upload to YouTube
2. **SEO Optimization**: Title, description, tags, thumbnail
3. **Scheduling**: Schedule for optimal time
4. **Cross-Platform**: Share on LinkedIn, Twitter, email

**Total Time per Video**: 5-10 hours
