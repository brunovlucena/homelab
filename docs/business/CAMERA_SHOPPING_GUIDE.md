## üö® Common Pitfalls to Avoid

1. **Wrong AI Features**: Verify camera has specific AI (food detection, not just people counting)
2. **Language Barrier**: Use Alibaba.com (English) vs. Taobao (Chinese only)
3. **Customs/Duties**: Request DDP shipping (Delivered Duty Paid)
4. **Warranty**: Confirm warranty covers your country
5. **Compatibility**: Verify RTSP/ONVIF support for integration
6. **Power**: Ensure PoE+ switch can power all cameras
7. **Bandwidth**: Calculate network requirements (100Mbps per camera)
