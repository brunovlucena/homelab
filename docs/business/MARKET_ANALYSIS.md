## Market Challenges (Our Opportunity)

### 1. Implementation Complexity
**Problem**: Integrating AI agents with existing legacy systems is complex
- Fragmented ecosystems (ERP, CRM, data lakes, cloud platforms)
- Requires structured data pipelines
- Seamless orchestration across systems

**Our Solution**: Pre-built integrations, event-driven architecture, API-first design

### 2. High Costs & Unclear ROI
**Problem**: 40% of agentic AI projects canceled by 2027 due to:
- High implementation costs
- Unclear business outcomes
- Long time-to-value

**Our Solution**: Production-ready platform, rapid deployment (days vs. months), clear ROI metrics

### 3. Security Risks
**Problem**: 
- 80% of organizations experience AI agents acting outside boundaries
- Non-human identities (NHIs) expanding attack surface
- Unauthorized access and data handling

**Our Solution**: Built-in security (zero-trust, RBAC, audit logging), HIPAA/SOC2 compliance

### 4. Skill Gaps
**Problem**: 
- Specialized skills required (AI development, data science, system integration)
- Difficulty recruiting and retaining talent
- Hinders scaling of AI initiatives

**Our Solution**: No-code/low-code platform, pre-built agents, comprehensive documentation

### 5. Data Quality Issues
**Problem**: 
- AI agents require high-quality, structured data
- Poor data quality leads to inaccurate outputs
- Reduced trust in AI systems

**Our Solution**: Data validation, quality checks, knowledge graph for context
