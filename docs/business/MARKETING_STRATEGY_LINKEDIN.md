LinkedIn is the primary platform for B2B marketing in the AI automation space. This strategy focuses on **thought leadership**, **educational content**, and **direct engagement** to build authority and generate qualified leads for our AI agent automation services.

1. **Build Authority**: Establish Bruno Lucena as a thought leader in AI automation

### 2. Carousel Posts (5-10 slides)

**Best Practices**:
- First slide: Hook/question
- Middle slides: Value/education
- Last slide: CTA/resource
- Use visuals: Charts, diagrams, screenshots
- Include data and metrics

**Topics**:
- "5 Ways AI Agents Transform Healthcare Operations"
- "ROI of Restaurant AI: Real Numbers"
- "Architecture Patterns for Production AI Agents"
- "HIPAA Compliance in AI Systems: A Guide"

### 3. LinkedIn Articles (1,500-2,500 words)

**Structure**:
- Compelling headline
- Introduction with hook
- Main content (3-5 sections with subheadings)
- Conclusion with CTA
- Relevant images, charts, code examples

**Article Topics**:
- "Building HIPAA-Compliant AI Agents: A Complete Guide"
- "The Future of Restaurant Automation: Beyond POS Systems"
- "DevOps Automation with AI Agents: Reducing MTTR by 50%"
- "Smart Contract Security: How AI Agents Prevent Exploits"
- "ROI Calculator: How Much Can AI Automation Save Your Business?"

**Publishing Frequency**: 1-2 articles per month

### 4. Video Content (1-3 minutes)

**Types**:
- **Quick Tips**: 60-second automation tips
- **Explainer Videos**: How AI agents work
- **Case Studies**: Customer success stories (animated)
- **Live Sessions**: Q&A, demos, interviews

**Best Practices**:
- Captions/subtitles (many watch without sound)
- Thumbnail with compelling text
- Hook in first 3 seconds
- Clear CTA at end

**Publishing Frequency**: 2-3 videos per month

### 5. Polls & Interactive Content

**Poll Topics**:
- "What's your biggest automation challenge?"
- "Which industry needs AI automation most?"
- "ROI expectation from automation: <6 months, 6-12 months, >12 months?"

**Publishing Frequency**: 1 poll per week

## Best Practices & Tips

### Profile Optimization

1. **Professional Headline**: "AI Automation Engineer | Building Production-Ready AI Agents for Healthcare, Restaurants & E-Commerce"
2. **Summary**: Story-driven, value-focused, keyword-rich
3. **Experience**: Quantify results (e.g., "Reduced MTTR by 50%", "Increased ROI by 200%")
4. **Skills**: List 50+ relevant skills, get endorsements
5. **Recommendations**: Request 5-10 recommendations
6. **Featured Section**: Link to best work, articles, case studies

### Content Best Practices

1. **Post at Optimal Times**: Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM or 12-1 PM (your timezone)
2. **Use Native Video**: Higher engagement than external links
3. **Include CTAs**: Clear call-to-action in every post
4. **Respond Quickly**: Reply to comments within 2 hours
5. **Be Authentic**: Share failures, lessons learned, behind-the-scenes

### Engagement Best Practices

1. **Personalize Connection Requests**: Always add a note
2. **Add Value First**: Don't pitch immediately
3. **Follow Up**: Engage multiple times before asking for a meeting
4. **Share Others' Content**: Build relationships, not just followers
